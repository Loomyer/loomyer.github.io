Loom

A loom is a device that allows you to weave cloth. It lets you take a simple thing like string and make it into something useful like clothing. It is a very powerful tool that lets you create things. Things that can earn you a living. It's a good metaphor for our education.

Lumière

Loomyer aims to shine some light (lumière) into your life by providing you several tools that allow you to create meaningful things that can enhance your life.



Loomyer provides an amazing experience for students to gain enlightenment through knowledge of the world we live in. Knowledge that they can apply to benefit themselves and earn a living.

How it works

Video Lectures & Calls



Watch video lectures previously prepared by the best instructors and join scheduled (and recorded) video conferences to ask questions and discuss the topic.

Homework & Graded Quizzes



Answer homework questions and graded quizes covering the discussed material by the instructor.

Practical Applications



Apply acquired knowledge in useful ways to illustrate the importance of covered material and reinforces the learnt material.

A Message

Hi, I’m Saad. I started Loomyer because I believe education is critical to improving people’s lives.I was very fortunate to have an amazing education growing up. I grew up in Cairo, Egypt and went to The International School Of Choueifat in Cairo. Choueifat has a very strong curriculum that prepared me for my college education. I was able to get accepted into George Mason University in Fairfax, Virginia where I got my B.S. in Systems Engineering and then my M.S. in Operations Research.My early education prepared me to pursue and better myself through higher education.
Many are not as fortunate. Loomyer aims to provide high quality education to the masses. This means it is available at affordable prices and easily accessible at all times. As much as it helped, my early education was still at a very theoretical level. Loomyer aims to change that by providing an easy way to show students why what they are learning matters and how it can help them in their daily lives.
I hope that Loomyer can revolutionize education worldwide by empowering our youth to believe in themselves, reach for the stars and get there.
